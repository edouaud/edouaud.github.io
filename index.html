What this project was about
During the Space App Camp 2021, our team developed in 8 weeks a mobile app prototype that helps smallholder farmers prepare for an upcoming growing season by predicting annual rainfall. What this project was about
I fulfilled a childhood dream of mixing aeronautics and computer science when I worked for 3 months on a startup project to create a Vertical Take Off and Landing Drone for 2 personnes to address the future on-demand urban air transportation:
  • Studied the market and already existing projects (CityAirbus, Lilium, Ehang, Volocopter, Cora, Uber Elevate, Sure Fly, etc).
  • Researched technical constraints and costs of electric motors, Flight Controller, ESC (Electronic Speed Controller), etc.
  • Studied Air Traffic Control legal constraints: aircraft categories, pilot licenses, restricted areas, conditions to fly over towns, etc.
After 3 months of work, a no-go decision has been taken because of too large financing needs. Yes I'm a big fan of the Lean Startup principles including fail fast ;-)

What this job was about
Strategy / Business / Consulting / HR
In 2007 I created a startup in an emerging field: Enterprise Social Software. I then turned the company into one of the leading French Enterprise Social Network and Ideation vendors with a team of 15 very tallented colleagues. The management of the team was done with agile (scrum) and Lean Startup methodologies.

In addition to strategy, as the CEO, my mission also included the communication and marketing: I planned and implemented social media and content marketing strategies, resulting in a 500% increase in weekly leads within 10 months.

I also advised clients on designing and planning their innovation strategy through consulting.

R&D and Product Management
I led the internal research program on psychosociological behaviour and designed our very first product from A to Z:

  • Selected the adequate technologies and providers,
  • Produced and tested first UX and UI,
  • Coded back-end and front-end of the MVP (then the versions sold were coded by people much more talented than me :-)
